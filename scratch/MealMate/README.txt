# MealMate User Data Directory

This directory contains your unified user data for the Antigravity weekly menu planner.

Folders & Files:
- preferences.json: Your active dietary preferences (cuisine, allergies, exclusions) synced from email quizzes.
- YYYY.MM.DD-MENU/: Weekly menus approved by the users.
- YYYY-MONTH/: Monthly archives of approved menus.

Note: Approved menus are automatically archived on the 1st of every month and deleted after 180 days.
